Shipping & Delivery Information

Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly as possible. Once your order is placed, it will be processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days.

  • Local Shipping: Delivery within 3-5 business days.
  • International Shipping: Delivery within 7-14 business days.
  • Shipping Charges: Free shipping on orders over $50. A flat rate of $5 applies to orders below $50.
Delivery Process

We ensure a hassle-free delivery experience. Our delivery process includes:

  1. Order Confirmation via Email.
  2. Packing and Dispatching from our warehouse.
  3. Real-time tracking link provided to your registered email or phone number.
  4. On-time delivery by our trusted courier partners.

If you face any issues during delivery, please contact our support team for assistance.

Returns and Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your order, you can request a return or refund within 14 days of delivery. Please note:

  • Items must be in their original condition with all tags intact.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after the return is received.
  • For detailed instructions, visit our Return Policy page.
